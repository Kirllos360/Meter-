# CASHIER GUIDE

## Daily Collections

### 1. Receive Payment
1. Go to **Payments** → **New Payment**
2. Search customer by name, code, or phone
3. Select unpaid invoices (system shows oldest-first)
4. Enter payment amount
5. Select method: `Cash`, `Bank Transfer`, `Card`, `Cheque`, `Mobile Wallet`
6. Add receipt reference number (optional)
7. Click **Submit**

### 2. Issue Receipt
1. After payment, click **Print Receipt**
2. Receipt shows: payment number, customer, amount, date, method
3. Give printed receipt to customer

### 3. Reverse Payment
1. Go to **Payments** → find the payment
2. Click **Reverse** (requires supervisor approval)
3. Enters reason for reversal
4. System reverses allocations and updates invoice balances

### 4. End of Day
1. Go to **Collections Dashboard**
2. Verify today's collections match cash register
3. Export daily collection report (CSV)

## Payment Methods

| Method | Processing Time | Notes |
|--------|----------------|-------|
| Cash | Instant | Issue receipt immediately |
| Bank Transfer | 1-2 business days | Verify before confirming |
| Card | Instant | POS integration required |
| Cheque | 3-5 business days | Hold until cleared |
| Mobile Wallet | Instant | Supported providers |

## Common Issues

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Customer not found | Search by phone number or national ID |
| Invoice shows paid but customer disagrees | Check payment allocations |
| Wrong amount entered | Reverse and re-enter |
